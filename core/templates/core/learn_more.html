Our Mission

Every photo you take, every species you identify, and every location you record helps scientists, researchers, and conservationists better understand and protect our planet's incredible biodiversity.

How It Works

SightTrack combines the power of computer vision, machine learning, and citizen science to create a comprehensive wildlife monitoring system:

1. Capture

Take photos of wildlife and plants using your smartphone camera through our intuitive app interface.

2. Identify

Our AI instantly analyzes your photos to identify species, providing detailed information and confidence scores.

3. Verify

Expert naturalists and community members can verify identifications to ensure data accuracy.

4. Contribute

Your verified observations become part of a global database used by researchers and conservationists.
