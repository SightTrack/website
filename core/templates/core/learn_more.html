Our Mission

SightTrack empowers everyday people to become citizen scientists, contributing valuable data to wildlife conservation efforts worldwide. Through our innovative platform, we're building the most comprehensive database of biodiversity observations ever created.

Every photo you take, every species you identify, and every location you record helps scientists, researchers, and conservationists better understand and protect our planet's incredible biodiversity.

How It Works

SightTrack provides a scientific workflow that guides citizen scientists through systematic biodiversity monitoring and conservation action:

1

2

4
